Description of : text

When discussing different types of textoptions in HTML there is quite a few different options to use.
For example if you want to provide a header to your webpage with related subheaders as in Microsoft Word
you can use the header-tags (i.e <h1>, <h2>, <h3> etc).

When you are done with your headers you can start typing the actual text you want on your webpage.
Two different ways of entering text on your webpage is either a normal paragraph which is used by the <p>-tag.
When you have posted some paragraphs and you need to cite a scientific paper for example the blockquote is a suitable option.
